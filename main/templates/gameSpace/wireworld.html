Wireworld

Brian Silvermans Wireworld is the resulting projection of the game of life that was developed with the intention of simulating electronic circuits. In this game, cells can be in one of 4 states:

  • Empty (White),
  • Electron head (Blue),
  • Electron tail (Red),
  • Conductor / Wire (Yellow).

The behaviour of cells is determined as follows:

  • empty → empty,
  • electron head → electron tail,
  • electron tail → conductor,
  • conductor → electron head if exactly one or two of the neighbouring cells are electron heads, otherwise remains conductor.
